About Biking First

Get best Mountain Biking deals

For the last thirteen years, the team behind Biking First has been exploring Nepal and the Himalaya on Mountain Bikes. We know where to take you for the best mountain bike adventures. We love biking and firmly believe that this way of travel not only energizes, stimulates and excites your senses, but it allows you to escape the well trodden tourist trails and experience the local people and culture first hand. Our love of cycling and passion for adventure and real life biking tour experiences is what drives us to deliver high quality biking tours to all levels of rider wishing to explore this beautiful region.
